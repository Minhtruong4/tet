The Dark Art of Silver Casting

Every piece that leaves our atelier begins as a sketch drawn at midnight. We believe the best ideas emerge in the silence of the night — when distractions fall away and imagination takes over. From that sketch, our silversmiths create a wax master model, refine it by hand, and then cast it in molten 925 sterling silver using the lost-wax casting method, unchanged for centuries.

The result is not just an accessory. It is an artifact — carrying the weight of the hands that shaped it and the vision of those who wore the night as their canvas.
