Est. 2019 — Born in darkness

We Craft for Those Who Wear the Night

Oculus Noctis — Latin for "Eye of the Night" — was founded in 2019 by a silversmith who grew up under the shadow of Gothic cathedrals and was drawn equally to the beauty of darkness and the brilliance of silver.

What began as a small workshop producing custom rings for a handful of clients has grown into a full brand with sixteen signature pieces and a community of customers who share a common aesthetic: bold, precise, and unapologetically dark.

Every piece we make is cast from 925 sterling silver in our atelier. No mass production. No shortcuts. Each ring, pendant, bracelet, and earring is individually finished by hand, inspected, and packaged with care before it reaches you.

What We Stand For

Three principles guide everything we create at Oculus Noctis.

Authentic Materials

We use only 925 sterling silver — verified, hallmarked, and sourced from certified suppliers. Our customers never compromise on what touches their skin.

Handmade Precision

Every piece passes through human hands at each stage — from the wax master model to the final polish. Machines assist; artisans decide.

Dark Elegance

We believe that beauty lives in contrast — between light and shadow, silver and black, delicate and bold. Our designs hold that tension intentionally.
